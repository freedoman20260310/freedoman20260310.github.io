# SoundHub Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-24

SoundHub respects your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles data during playback, subtitles, offline downloads, and third-party service connections.

## Information We Collect

SoundHub stores the playback queue, playback progress, playlists, theme preferences, subtitle settings, diagnostic logs, and imported or offline audio files on your device.

## Baidu Netdisk Authorization

When you choose to sign in to Baidu Netdisk, SoundHub uses the Baidu Open Platform Client ID you entered to start authorization and stores the access token in secure device storage so it can browse and play the Netdisk audio you choose.

## Subtitles and Cloud Services

Local transcription processes audio on your device. If you choose cloud transcription, background cloud jobs, or language model correction, SoundHub sends the necessary audio segments or subtitle text to the service provider you configured in Settings.

## Third-Party Services

Baidu Netdisk, cloud Whisper providers, language model services, and any custom endpoints you configure process requests according to their own privacy policies. Only connect services you trust.

## Data Controls

You can disconnect Baidu Netdisk, clear diagnostic logs, delete subtitles, clear offline downloads, or delete the app to remove data stored on this device.

## Children's Privacy

SoundHub is not designed for children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Policy Updates

We may update this policy as features change. Store releases should also provide a publicly accessible privacy policy webpage.

## Contact Us

For privacy-related support, contact us through the support method listed in App Store Connect.

# SoundHub 隐私政策

最后更新：2026-06-24

SoundHub 尊重你的隐私。本政策说明 App 在播放、字幕、离线下载和第三方服务连接中如何处理数据。

## 我们收集的信息

SoundHub 会在本机保存播放队列、播放进度、播放列表、主题偏好、字幕设置、诊断日志和你导入或离线下载的音频文件。

## 百度网盘授权

当你主动登录百度网盘时，SoundHub 会使用你填写的百度开放平台 Client ID 发起授权，并将访问令牌保存在设备安全存储中，用于浏览和播放你选择的网盘音频。

## 字幕与云端服务

本地转写会在设备上处理音频。选择云端转写、后台云端任务或语言模型修正时，SoundHub 会把必要的音频片段或字幕文本发送到你在设置中配置的服务提供商。

## 第三方服务

百度网盘、云端 Whisper、语言模型服务和你配置的自定义 Endpoint 会按照各自的隐私政策处理请求。请只连接你信任的服务。

## 数据控制

你可以在 App 内断开百度网盘、清空诊断日志、删除字幕、清空离线下载，或删除 App 以移除本机保存的数据。

## 儿童隐私

SoundHub 不面向儿童设计，也不会有意收集儿童个人信息。

## 政策更新

我们可能会随着功能变化更新本政策。发布到商店的版本应同时提供可公开访问的隐私政策网页。

## 联系我们

如需隐私相关支持，请通过 App Store Connect 中填写的支持方式联系我们。
